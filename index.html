Lyuxin (David) Zhang

I'm an undergraduate and accelerated master's student at the University of Pennsylvania, studying Computer Science & Mathematics (B.A.) and Data Science (M.S.E.), graduating in 2027.

I work on data-centric machine learning, seeking principled approaches to training, understanding, and evaluating LLMs. At UPenn, I am fortunate to work with Surbhi Goel and Dan Roth, and have collaborated with Eric Wong and Chris Callison-Burch.
